Receipe for Gambas

Description

This is receipe for making Gambas. It is good food to eat with pasta or steak

Ingredients

  • 12 shrimps
  • 16 garlics
  • High quality olive oils
  • Peppers
  • Onions 1/2

Steps

  1. Marinate shrimps with olive oil. Add salt and pepper.
  2. Put plenty of olive oil into a pan and heated.
  3. Cut heads of shrimp and cook it in medium heat.
  4. When shrimps start to turn red, take heads out and put onions and garlics. Add salt and pepper.
  5. After 10 minutes or so, add shrimp and cook each side for 3 minutes.

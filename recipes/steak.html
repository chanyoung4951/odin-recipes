Receipe for Steak

Description

This is a guide for cooking a perfecct steak using a reverse-searing method.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt and peppers
  • Garlics
  • Butter
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Marinate steak with salt and pepper and put it in a fridge for 4 hours.
  2. Put steak in the oven, until it reaches internal temperture of 115F.
  3. Once internal temperture reach 115F, cook the steak on an iron cast.
  4. Cook each side for about a minute. Add butter, thyme, and garlic.

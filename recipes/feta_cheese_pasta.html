Receipe for Feta Cheese Pasta

Description

This is a easy way to make a quality pasta in oven.

Ingredients

  • 600g Cheery Tomatoes
  • Salt and peppers
  • 2 Garlics
  • Handful of basil
  • 350g Penne Pasta
  • 1 Cup of quality extra virgin olive oil
  • 200g Feta Cheese

Steps

  1. Put washed cherry tomato on baking dish. Put olive oils, salt, peppers, and feta cheese.
  2. Put dish in the oven and cook it for 40 minutes at 350F.
  3. Put pasta in boiled water and cook it al dante.
  4. Put cooked pasta into baking dish. And put crushed garlic and fresh basil.
